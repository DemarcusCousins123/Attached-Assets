Here is an **improved copy-paste prompt** you can give to a web app builder like Lovable, Bolt.new, or Replit that includes **the deal analyzer + a contact/communication system** for customers to send you car requests.

# Copy-Paste Prompt for Web App Builder

Build a modern web application called **Apex Auto Advisory**.
The platform helps people determine whether a car is a good deal and also allows them to contact our advisory team for personalized vehicle research.

The design should look like a professional startup platform with a **dark blue, white, and gray color scheme** and a clean modern UI similar to a fintech analytics dashboard.

The platform should include both **automated deal analysis tools** and **direct communication with our advisory team**.

---

# Core Feature 1: Car Deal Analyzer

On the homepage, include a tool where users can paste a car listing URL or manually enter vehicle information.

Input fields should include:

Vehicle Make
Vehicle Model
Year
Mileage
Price
Location
Listing URL (optional)

After submission, generate a **Deal Analysis Report** showing:

* Estimated fair market value range
* Comparison to similar listings
* A Deal Score from 1–100
* Labels such as:

  * Great Deal
  * Fair Price
  * Slightly Overpriced
  * Overpriced
* Recommended negotiation price range
* Short explanation of whether the deal is good or bad

Display the results in a clean dashboard with charts and a clear score indicator.

---

# Core Feature 2: Vehicle Request System

Create a section where users can **request help finding a car**.

The form should allow users to submit:

Budget range
Preferred make and model
Vehicle type (SUV, sedan, truck, etc.)
Maximum mileage
Preferred year range
Location
Additional notes

Example notes:
“I’m looking for a reliable SUV under $25k with good resale value.”

After submission, display a confirmation message:

“Our advisory team will research vehicles that match your criteria and send you recommendations within 24–48 hours.”

The request should appear in an **admin dashboard** so our team can view and respond to requests.

---

# Core Feature 3: Contact & Messaging

Create a **Contact Our Advisors** page with the following options:

### Email Contact Form

Fields:
Name
Email
Subject
Message

Messages should be sent to the business email inbox.

### SMS/Text Contact Option

Allow users to optionally enter their phone number so the team can reply via text message.

Include a message:

“You may receive a text message from our advisory team regarding your request.”
